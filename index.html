About

EMIT Studios bridges product design and enterprise delivery

EMIT Studios is the consultancy platform for Eudonni Moricom. The business is built around a practical gap seen in many organisations. Design teams often stop at concepts. Delivery teams often inherit products without enough user focus. EMIT closes that gap.

Business Synopsis

The studio designs digital products that support real work. That includes mobile applications, internal platforms, commercial dashboards, CRM systems, onboarding portals, and workflow integrations. Projects are shaped for environments where compliance, operational reliability, and clarity matter.

This work spans healthcare, pharmaceutical, financial services, and consumer technology. The value of EMIT Studios sits in combining user experience design with the discipline needed to deliver systems inside real organisations.

What Makes EMIT Different
  • One partner across discovery, UX, interface design, dashboard design, workflow mapping, and implementation support.
  • Strong experience in regulated, operational, and multi stakeholder environments.
  • Design decisions grounded in how teams actually work, not only how screens look.
  • A portfolio that shows both client facing products and internal business systems.

Process

How EMIT Studios works

The process is structured to keep design connected to delivery, business goals, and real world implementation.

01

Discover

Review the business need, user context, workflow constraints, and technology environment. Clarify what success needs to look like.

02

Design

Create journeys, interface architecture, dashboards, systems views, and product concepts that support the work users need to do.

03

Align

Work across stakeholders, technical teams, and business owners so requirements, priorities, and implementation decisions stay joined up.

04

Deliver

Support build, rollout, optimisation, and operational handover so the final output works inside the organisation, not only in presentation.
